About

Katanas are our most popular weapon, known worldwide as the trusty main blade of the Samurai. However, appropriation in Western media has muddled their true historical use and styling. Peruse our collections and order from reputable forges to discover the actual applications of this classic blade. Premade, custom, and collaborations available.

The Tsurugi, also known as the Ken, was a popular main or short sword unique for the sharp edges on both sides. The Tsurugi was most commonly used prior to the 900s A.D. as the main weapon, but upon the advent of curved, single edged swords like Katanas, they fell out of fashion, only in use in religious shrines. Order premade or custom Tsurugi offerings from our partner forges.

Naginatas, the renowned Japanese polearm ubiquotous alongside calvary-clearing soldiers in the battlefield, order-keeping Samurai and warrior monks, and self-defending high-class women, is another best seller on Swords of Japan. Swipe through collections from various forges, including examples adorned in ornamental fittings, battle-ready fittings, and everything in between.

SPECIAL FITTINGS

Swords can be customized to be used in religious settings, like in Buddhist and Shinto temples, or for edge-to-edge battle practice, tatami competition, and Iiado, in which parts like the Tsuka (leather/silk/nylon wrap above the ray skin) and Menuki (metal handle ornament) are more securely tightened, the Tsuba (hand guard) and Sageo (mounting string) are strengthened, and the Saya (sheath/scabbard) is lined with more layers of Buffalo horn for more durable draws.
